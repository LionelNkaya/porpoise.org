RACECAR vehicles

RC Controlled off-road racecar with Arduino or Raspberry Pi computer, 12 LEDs, range finder 1800 mAh Nickel Metal Hydride Battery.
Rugged vehicle, off-road suspension, approximately 20 mph, computer programmable.
All vehicles with curriculum and training material on USB thumb drive. All vehicles can be disassembled and returned to kit form for subsequent classes.

  • Racecar Arduino Intro - Kit $250

    • Nitro Exceed 1/10 scale 4-wheel drive, off-road racecar

    • Arduino Computer, 4 LEDs lights, Range Finder, 1800mAh Nickel Metal Hydride Battery, 9VDC Battery and holder

    • Set of Instructional slides and programs on 16GByte Thumb Drive

  • Upgrade to Racecar Arduino $100

    • NiCad battery power distribution cable for Arduino computer and Servo Driver

      Servo driver, voltage converter, 8 pre-wired LEDs.

    • To upgrade: Drill holes for the 8 LEDs, servo driver, switch and servo to LEDs. About 15 total.

      • Remove the 9 VDC battery holder, install and wire the battery interface cable, LEDs, servo driver, switch and converter.

      • You would install the Arduino Computer into its case if desired. Instruction for modification provided.
